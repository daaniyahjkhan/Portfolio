Shoulder-press-counter

Shoulder-Press-Counter is an intelligent fitness tool that uses MediaPipe’s pose detection technology to accurately count your shoulder press repetitions in real time. By analyzing your body movements through a webcam or video feed, it automatically detects each completed shoulder press and keeps track of your reps, helping you maintain proper form and monitor your workout progress without needing manual counting. This makes your exercise routine more efficient and engaging, providing instant feedback so you can focus on your fitness goals.
